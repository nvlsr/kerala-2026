**Here is a catalog of ~28 distinct narratives** drawn from post-election analyses (primarily May 4-6, 2026), news outlets, commentators, and X discussions. ### 1. Strong Anti-Incumbency Wave Against LDF After Decade in Power
**Claim:** Kerala voters traditionally alternate fronts every 5 years; after 10 years of LDF rule and despite 2021's COVID exception, fatigue, governance issues, and desire for change delivered a decisive UDF mandate, ending the Left's last state government.

**Source(s):**  
- The Hindu (May 2026); Times of India (May 2026)  
- The Quint (Aditya Menon, May 4, 2026)

**Evidence strength:** Multiple agreeing  
**Theme:** anti-incumbency  
**Testable with seat-level data?** Yes — by examining swings in LDF strongholds vs. marginal seats and correlation with incumbency duration.
