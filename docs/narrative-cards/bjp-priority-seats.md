# BJP's priority seats — what was placed, what came back

BJP did not officially publish a priority list. The 10 seats below are widely understood to be where the party placed its most-winnable candidates — sometimes informally including Chathannoor and sometimes not, possibly a deliberate ambiguity to avoid signalling other seats as less prioritised. Treat this list as best-available, not authoritative.

## Summary

Of 10 priority seats, BJP won 2 — both in the Trivandrum belt (Nemom, Kazhakoottam). Outside Trivandrum, all 5 scattered priorities lost; three of them slipped backward despite marquee candidates (Manjeshwar −1.6pp, Palakkad −1.8pp, Thrissur −8.0pp). The Trivandrum belt accounted for 5 of 10 priority placements and produced the only conversions from priority to win. **Among the seats where BJP put their best candidates, the Trivandrum belt is the only zone that delivered.** Chathannoor was the third BJP win and was not a clean priority — its inclusion was inconsistent — making it more of an unexpected belt-spillover gain than a planned conversion.
